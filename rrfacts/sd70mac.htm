SD70MAC

The EMD SD70MAC is the first commercially successful AC traction locomotive in North America. It was first produced for Burlington Northern in quanity in 1994. By 2000 BNSF had over 800 SD70MACs. Although primarily used for the demanding coal train service they can be found pulling all types of trains.

  • Horsepower: 4,000
  • Weight: 415,000 lbs
  • Gear Ratio: 85:16
  • Wheel diameter: 42"
  • Height: 15' 9"
  • Length: 74 ft
  • Width: 10' 1"
  • Fuel: 5,000 gallons
  • Lube Oil: 436 gal.
  • Cooling Water: 276 gal
  • Engine: EMD 710G3B, 16 cylinder with EFI
  • Cost in 1994: $1.93 million
