GN Signals

1967

RuleAspectNameIndication
240AStopStop before any part of train or engine passes the signal
240BStop and ProceedStop before any part of train or engine passes the signal then proceed at Restricted Speed through entire block
240CFlashing Stop and ProceedStop. Block occupied. Proceed at Restricted Speed
240DApproachProceed prepared to to stop before any part of train or engine passes the next signal. Trains exceeding 40 mph must immediately reduce to that speed
240EClearProceed
240FAdvance ApproachProceed prepared to pass next signal not exceeding 40 mph
240GApproach DivergingApproach next signal prepared to proceed on diverging route at prescribed speed
240KDiverging Advance ApproachProceed on diverging route at prescribed speed prepared to pass next signal not exceeding 40 mph
240LDiverging ApproachProceed on diverging route at prescribed speed prepared to stop before any part of train or engine passes the next signal
240MDiverging ClearProceed on diverging route at prescribed speed
240NRestrictingProceed at Restricted Speed
240PPermissiveProceed at Restricted Speed without stopping

Aspects 240C through 240N may be displayed on signals with or without a number plate on signal mast.
